02 · How It Works

Fast, specific, no noise.

We don't send a stack of resumes. We send the right candidates — after doing the technical vetting ourselves.

01

Scope the role together

We start with a short call to understand your stack, the problem you're solving, team structure, and what "good" looks like technically. No generic job description intake.

02

We vet, you choose

We identify and technically screen candidates from our Bay Area AI/ML network. You receive a short, curated shortlist — typically 2–3 candidates — within days, not weeks.

03

Contractor starts

Once you select, we handle contracting logistics and onboarding coordination. The contractor integrates with your team on your terms — short sprint, long project, or ongoing.
