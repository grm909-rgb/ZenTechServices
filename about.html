AI, Data & ERP Talent. On Your Terms.

Zentalix gives enterprise technology teams on-demand access to vetted specialists across two practices — AI and data, and Oracle and SAP.

On the AI side, we match practitioners to the need — whether that's an ML engineer who has trained and fine-tuned models, an LLM specialist building RAG pipelines, a data engineer designing lakehouse architecture, or an MLOps practitioner standing up ML platforms. On the Oracle and SAP side, we source experienced ERP consultants across Fusion Cloud, S/4HANA, FICO, HCM, and beyond. How We Operate

The principles we work by.

01

Specificity over scope

Excellence in a narrow set beats mediocrity across a broad range. AI and data staffing and Oracle and SAP staffing are our two domains — we don't stretch beyond that to win business.

02

Technical honesty

We call things what they are. If a consultant's AI expertise is surface-level, we say so. If a simpler approach will solve the problem, we'll say that too. Candor builds more trust than overselling.

03

Outcomes, not activities

Success means placing the right person quickly and having them stay. We vet upfront so you spend time evaluating fit — not filtering noise.

04

Lean by design

Speed, quality, and domain depth — not headcount growth. The value we provide comes from people who know the domain, not coordinators managing coordinators.

05

Right fit or no fit

If your need doesn't match what we do well, we'll say so directly rather than take the work and underdeliver. Reputation matters more than short-term revenue.

06

Consulting quality first

Two strong candidates beats a stack of average ones. Speed matters, but not at the expense of the technical bar. We screen upfront — you evaluate fit.
