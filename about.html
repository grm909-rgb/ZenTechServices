Mission

Why Zentalix.

Zentalix gives enterprise technology teams fast access to vetted specialists across two of the most in-demand domains in enterprise technology: AI and data, and Oracle and SAP. Both practices are run at the same standard — neither is a bolt-on.

On the AI and data side, we place practitioners with genuine hands-on depth: engineers who have trained models, built production RAG pipelines, shipped agentic systems, and designed ML platforms from the ground up. Not candidates with "AI" on a resume — people who have actually done the work.

On the Oracle and SAP side, we source experienced ERP consultants across the full module range — Fusion Cloud, S/4HANA, FICO, HCM, and beyond — giving enterprise programs the specialist depth they need to keep moving.

Most enterprise technology teams run AI initiatives and ERP programs at the same time. Managing separate staffing vendors for each adds friction and overhead. Zentalix handles both through a single relationship, with the same vetting standards and the same commitment to placing people who are genuinely qualified — not just available.

Technical credibility is earned, not claimed. We're specific about what we can deliver, honest about what we can't, and focused on engagements where we can genuinely move the needle.

How We Operate

The principles we work by.

01

Specificity over scope

We'd rather be excellent at a narrow set of things than mediocre across a broad range. Data and AI is our primary domain. Oracle and SAP staffing is a supporting capability. We don't stretch beyond that to win business.

02

Technical honesty

We won't describe a consultant as "AI experienced" because they've used ChatGPT. We won't pitch a fine-tuning project if off-the-shelf prompting will solve the problem. Buyers respect candor more than overselling.

03

Outcomes, not activities

We measure success by how quickly the right person is placed and how long they stay. A fast shortlist of weak candidates isn't a service. We do the vetting upfront so you spend time evaluating fit, not filtering noise.

04

Lean by design

We're focused on speed, quality, and deep domain expertise — not scale for its own sake. The value we provide comes from having the right people on every engagement: practitioners who know the domain, not coordinators managing coordinators.

05

Right fit or no fit

If a client's need doesn't match what we do well, we'll say so directly rather than take the work and underdeliver. The AI services market is small enough that reputation matters more than short-term revenue.

06

Consulting quality first

For staffing, we'd rather send a shortlist of two strong candidates than a volume of average ones. Speed matters, but not at the expense of the technical bar. We do the technical screening upfront — so you spend time evaluating fit, not filtering noise.
