Where great teams build AI that ships.

ZenTechServices was founded to close a real gap: organizations investing in AI need both the right people and the right execution — and most vendors offer only one.

01 · Mission

Why ZenTechServices exists.

Organizations building AI capabilities face two distinct problems. The first is talent — finding contractors with genuine AI/ML depth in a market full of surface-level generalists. The second is execution — getting a sophisticated AI system actually built and shipped, not just architected on a whiteboard.

Most firms solve one or the other. Staffing agencies fill seats. Consulting firms run projects. Neither does both well, and neither specializes deeply enough in the AI/ML domain to earn real technical credibility with the buyers who matter.

ZenTechServices was built to do both — with Data and AI as the primary domain, and Oracle and SAP contract staffing as a supporting capability for clients with enterprise ERP needs alongside their AI programs.

What we believe

Technical credibility is earned, not claimed. We don't describe every contractor as an "AI expert" or every project as a "transformation." We're specific about what we can deliver, honest about what we can't, and focused on clients where we can genuinely move the needle.

Who we work best with

Mid-market technology teams, financial services firms, and healthcare organizations that are serious about building real AI capability — not organizations looking for a vendor to rubber-stamp a strategy they've already decided on.

03 · How We Operate

The principles we work by.

01

Specificity over scope

We'd rather be excellent at a narrow set of things than mediocre across a broad range. Data and AI is our primary domain. Oracle and SAP staffing is a supporting capability. We don't stretch beyond that to win business.

02

Technical honesty

We won't describe a contractor as "AI experienced" because they've used ChatGPT. We won't pitch a fine-tuning project if off-the-shelf prompting will solve the problem. Buyers respect candor more than overselling.

03

Outcomes, not activities

For project delivery, we scope to deliverables — not hours logged. For staffing, we measure success by how quickly the right person is placed and how long they stay. Activity without results isn't a service.

04

Lean by design

We're not trying to become a 500-person consulting firm. The value we provide comes from domain focus, senior attention, and delivery quality — not from scale. Clients work with the people who know the domain, not account managers managing account managers.

05

Right fit or no fit

If a client's need doesn't match what we do well, we'll say so directly rather than take the work and underdeliver. The AI services market is small enough that reputation matters more than short-term revenue.

06

Contractor quality first

For staffing, we'd rather send a shortlist of two strong candidates than a volume of average ones. Speed matters, but not at the expense of the technical bar. We vet ourselves so clients don't have to.
